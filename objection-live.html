The Show

Objection Live! is where CJ goes live every Monday and pulls apart the biggest celebrity court cases in real time. New filings, new rulings, new drama - all of it gets broken down so you don't need a law degree to follow what's happening.

Everything comes from the actual filings. PACER, CourtListener, state court records, public documents. If it's in the docket, it's on the show.

Think of it as your front row seat to the cases everyone is talking about.
