✅ may 13 · paris jackson · $625K WIN

Paris Jackson v. MJ Estate · bonuses returned

Judge Mitchell L. Beckloff rules that $625K in bonuses paid by executors Branca & McClain to third-party law firms must be returned to the estate. Paris's team now demanding an additional $463K in accrued interest (May 18 filing). Paris: "a massive win" for the family. Per Deadline, TMZ.
✅ may 18 · ACQUITTED · $64M refund ordered

Shakira · Spain tax fraud · NOT GUILTY

Spain's National Court acquits Shakira of 2011 tax fraud after an 8-year fight. Authorities proved only 163 days in-country — short of the 183-day residency threshold. Court rejected claim her relationship with Piqué made her a resident. Treasury ordered to reimburse ~$64M in fines. Tax agency says it will appeal to the Supreme Court. Does NOT affect her 2023 plea deal for 2012-14 tax years. Per Variety, NPR.
🚨 may 13 · woods · prescription records IN

Florida judge approves the state's request to compel Woods's prescription drug records. Records under protective order — prosecutors, law enforcement, expert witnesses, defense only. Woods's medical info NOT made public. Defense atty Doug Duncan. Per People, iHeart.
✅ settled · timberlake + woods · tip theft

Justin Timberlake and Tiger Woods reach settlement in tip-theft lawsuit over their Manhattan sports bar T-Squared Social.
