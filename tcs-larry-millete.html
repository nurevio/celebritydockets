People v.
Larry Millete

May "Maya" Millete vanished from her Chula Vista home on Jan 7, 2021. Her body has never been found. Her husband Larry was charged with first-degree murder ten months later. The first jury hung. The case is back on the retrial track.

★ Citizens-only opinion. Everything here reflects publicly available evidence, court filings, and citizen analysis. Larry Millete is presumed innocent. Nothing on this page is a legal finding, court verdict, or determination of guilt.

★ Case Overview

Background

Three children. One mother. Zero body.

May "Maya" Millete — 39, mother of three — was last seen alive on January 7, 2021 at the Millete family home in Chula Vista, California. Her sister reported her missing two days later. The marriage was strained: Maya had told friends and family she was planning to file for divorce.

San Diego County prosecutors charged Larry Millete with first-degree murder + a deadly-weapon enhancement on October 19, 2021. Maya's remains have never been recovered. The case is built almost entirely on circumstantial evidence: text messages, online "spell-caster" purchases, and behavioral inconsistencies in the days before and after she disappeared.

The first jury trial ended in a hung jury. The retrial is now on track. Larry remains in custody.

★ Evidence on the Table

Prosecution's Case

The receipts the state is leaning on.

→ Spell-caster purchases. Larry allegedly paid online ritualists to "bind" Maya to him or to "make her leave the family alone." Receipts, emails, and account logs were recovered.

→ Firearm timeline. Texts and purchase records around handguns Larry acquired in the months before Maya vanished.

→ Behavior on Jan 7-8. What Larry did, who he called, what he searched on his phone in the hours after Maya was last seen.

→ The marriage's last weeks. Texts between Maya and her sisters and friends about leaving Larry. Plans for a divorce attorney consult that never happened.

Status · Retrial

The first jury hung. The DA refiled. Larry remains in custody. Retrial calendar firming up — we'll pin every motion, every ruling, every witness disclosure here as it lands.
