Nothing on this site constitutes legal advice. CJ is not a lawyer. The content on Celebrity Dockets is journalism and commentary based on publicly available court records. If you need legal advice, consult a licensed attorney in your jurisdiction.

Presumption of Innocence & Allegations

Criminal charges and civil allegations are unproven until a court rules. We describe what's in the record - who was charged, what's alleged, what's been ruled - not who is guilty. Use of names in active cases reflects reporting on public filings, not an accusation. "Pleaded not guilty" and other procedural status is noted wherever it appears in the record.
