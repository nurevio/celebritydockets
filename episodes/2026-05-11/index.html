★ rundown · McSweeney v. Cohen et al. · S.D.N.Y.

When do you settle?
The fight over the calendar.

A joint letter to Judge Gorenstein on Apr 30 (Doc 143) lays out the disagreement: defendants want the settlement conference now, McSweeney wants it after depositions. Plus Judge Liman's full Case Management Plan from Apr 15 — every deadline through trial.

★ apr 30, 2026 · doc 143 · joint letter to gorenstein

filed by jacob d. albertson · MSK · for cohen et al.

★ defendants' position (cohen et al.)

Settlement conference as soon as practicable. Near-term conference is in the interest of efficiency & judicial economy — resolves the case before the parties spend on discovery and summary judgment.

★ plaintiff's position (mcsweeney)

Settlement conference upon completion of depositions. Parties remain too far apart right now — a developed factual record will lead to a more productive and informed settlement discussion.

★ rundown · paris jackson v. mj estate executors

"Cars and fancy watches."
Paris escalates the fee fight.

Probate Case BP117321 · In re Estate of Michael Joseph Jackson, Deceased · L.A. Superior · Stanley Mosk. Paris is challenging executors John Branca & John McClain — not the will, the executors. The fight is about fees, gifts, and uninvested money.

Jun 25, 2009
Michael Jackson dies. Estate reportedly $500M in debt. Will directs all assets into a family trust for his three children + mother.
2009-2026
17 years of executor control. Branca & McClain take an insolvent estate to ~$2 billion. Per Paris's filings: $148M+ paid to executors through 2021.
Mar 10, 2026
Paris files redacted brief challenging executor fees + gifts. Read the PDF →
Mar 11, 2026
Court hearing — executors' attorney allegedly makes "strutting" remark.
Apr 6, 2026
Paris files new doc accusing executors of bullying and mocking her in court filings.
Apr 24, 2026
Michael biopic released — Universal · Antoine Fuqua · Jaafar Jackson stars. Paris's filings flag the $150M production as allegedly benefiting Branca personally.
May 2026
Hearing in Century City. Paris's filings target $600K+ in bonuses, "premium payments," and gifts including cars and fancy watches. Quote from her brief: "Mr. Branca's continued, fierce reliance on a deeply flawed fee application and his insistence on doling out gifts to his colleagues in violation of the Probate Code, this Court's order, and the siblings' objection."
★ why it matters · per The Conversation

Paris isn't fighting the will — she's fighting how the executors are running the estate. The estate is ~$2B; ~$464M is sitting uninvested. Executors get paid more when the estate stays open longer. The structural conflict is the story.

"Expansive spending might increase estate value — but could also benefit those managing it disproportionately, leaving less for beneficiaries who ultimately inherit remaining assets."
