★ Story 03 · What We're Watching
The questions for the jury

The three watch-fors.

After five years, what does the prosecution actually lean on? What's the defense's strongest argument? And what does the jury do when there's no body?

Watch · 01 Spell-caster vs. firearm. Does the state lean harder into the supernatural-purchase angle, or anchor on the gun timeline? The first one is sensational. The second one is concrete. Closing arguments will tell you which one they trusted.
Watch · 02 "No body, no DNA, no scene." Defense's go-to in a circumstantial case. The state has had five years to build the record around that gap. Reasonable doubt is a feeling — does it land?
Watch · 03 The petition that didn't help. Larry filed from custody to interrogate the same kids Maya's sister is now raising. The court's posture on that petition is going to leak into the rest of the case. Watch which judge calls it.
