Featured Dockets

The docket sleuths who pull, post, and surface celebrity court filings in real time. Most of what we cover here originates from the accounts listed below — credited, linked, and organized.

Why this page exists. Celebrity Dockets aggregates and frames public court filings, but the actual heavy lift — pulling PACER documents, screenshotting orders within minutes, live-tweeting hearings, surfacing local-court PDFs — is done by independent accounts. This is our running roster of who we follow and recommend you follow too. Outside links go directly to their feeds. If you'd like to be added or updated, ping @CelebDockets.

🔍 How We Use These Accounts

Every case page on Celebrity Dockets links the underlying court documents directly — PACER, CourtListener, Justia, DocumentCloud, court PDFs. Where the original surfacing came from a citizen-sleuth account, we credit the account by handle on the case page and in our show notes. This page is the running list of who those accounts are.

A few editorial notes:

  • Independent voices, not staff. None of these accounts work for Celebrity Dockets. We just read them and credit them.
  • Outside links go to their feeds. We don't republish their content here in full — we link out so they get the engagement.
  • Docket sleuths only. This page features accounts that pull, post, and surface court filings — not commentary or live-tweet accounts. Other voices may be credited inline on individual case pages.
  • If we're missing a docket sleuth: ping @CelebDockets.
